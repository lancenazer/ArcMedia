## KEY FACTS & DATA

### US Navy (Pacific Fleet)
- **Ships:** 200+ in Pacific
- **Carriers:** 5-6 nuclear carriers in region
- **Bases:** Japan, Guam, Hawaii
- **Personnel:** 150,000+

### People's Liberation Army Navy (PLAN)
- **Ships:** 350+ (largest navy by number)
- **Submarines:** 60+ (including nuclear)
- **Bases:** South China Sea, mainland
- **Missiles:** DF-21D "carrier killer"

## TALKING POINTS

1. **The Numbers Game**
   - China has MORE ships (but US has MORE tonnage)
   - US has better technology, experience
   - Numbers don't tell whole story

2. **The Geography**
   - US bases: Guam, Japan, Hawaii - stretched
   - China: Home territory advantage
   - 3,000 km from Chinese coast = danger zone

3. **The "Carrier Killer"**
   - DF-21D missile - can hit carriers
   - US concern #1
   - But has it been tested?

4. **Who Would Win?**
   - Near China: China advantage
   - Far from China: US advantage
   - It's complicated
