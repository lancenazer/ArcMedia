## KEY FACTS & DATA

### The 5 Cities (Ranked by Scale/Ambition)

#### #5: Masdar City, UAE
- **Location:** Abu Dhabi
- **Type:** Sustainability/Zero Carbon
- **Size:** 6 km²
- **Status:** Partially occupied since 2010, expanding
- **Focus:** 100% renewable energy, no carbon footprint

#### #4: Xiong'an, China  
- **Location:** 100km SW of Beijing
- **Type:** Smart City / Tech Hub
- **Size:** 1,200 km² (largest in China)
- **Cost:** $580 billion+ planned
- **Status:** Under construction since 2017, partial operation

#### #3: Neom (The Line), Saudi Arabia
- **Location:** Northwestern Saudi Arabia
- **Type:** Linear city / AI-integrated
- **Size:** 26,500 km² (entire region)
- **Cost:** $500 billion+
- **Status:** Construction ongoing

#### #2: Bilim, India (Amrita Smart City)
- **Location:** Kerala, India
- **Type:** Self-sustaining smart city
- **Size:** 500+ acres
- **Focus:** Education, sustainability
- **Status:** Operational phases

#### #1: Songdo, South Korea
- **Location:** Incheon, South Korea
- **Type:** Fully integrated smart city
- **Size:** 6 km² (fully built)
- **Cost:** $40 billion
- **Status:** Complete, inhabited since 2015
