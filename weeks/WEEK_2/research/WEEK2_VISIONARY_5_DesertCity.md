## KEY FACTS & DATA

### The Line (Neom Component)
- **Location:** Northwestern Saudi Arabia, Tabuk region
- **Length:** 170 kilometers (105 miles)
- **Height:** 500 meters (1,640 feet)
- **Width:** 200 meters
- **Cost:** Estimated $500 billion+ for full Neom (The Line alone: $100-200 billion)
- **Capacity:** 9 million residents planned
- **Completion Target:** 2030 (initial sections)

### What Makes It Unique
- No roads, no cars - ONLY walking/rail transit
- Powered by 100% renewable energy
- Climate-controlled (no AC needed)
- Built in a straight line for efficiency
- Year-round comfortable temperatures

### Project History
- **Announced:** 2017 at Future Investment Initiative
- **Construction Start:** 2022
- **Status (2026):** First sections operational/exterior complete
