## KEY FACTS & DATA

### The Project
- **Name:** Multiple projects under "Infrastructure Investment and Jobs Act" (IIJA)
- **Total Value:** $1.2 trillion (largest infrastructure investment in US history)
- **Timeframe:** 2021-2026 initial spending phase
- **Key Focus Areas:** Roads, bridges, broadband, ports, airports, clean energy

### Major Projects Included
1. **Hydrogen Hub Development** - $8 billion
2. **Port Infrastructure** - $17 billion
3. **Broadband Expansion** - $65 billion
4. **Electric Vehicle Infrastructure** - $7.5 billion
5. **Amtrak Modernization** - $66 billion

### China Context
- Belt and Road Initiative: $1 trillion+ invested globally
- China's infrastructure spending: ~6% of GDP annually
- US infrastructure spending: ~2.5% of GDP historically
