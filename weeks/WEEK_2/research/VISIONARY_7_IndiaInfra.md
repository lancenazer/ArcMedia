## KEY FACTS & DATA

### India's Infrastructure Push (2024-2026)
- **Investment:** $100+ billion annually
- **Goal:** Become 3rd largest economy by 2030
- **Key Projects:** Mumbai-Delhi expressway, bullet train, new airports

### Major Projects
1. **Mumbai-Nagpur Expressway** - $12 billion, 700km
2. **Delhi-Mumbai Expressway** - $13 billion, 1,350km (world's longest)
3. **Ayodhya Airport** - $270 million, built in 20 months
4. **Borendra Port** - $8 billion, India's largest
5. **High-Speed Rail (Bullet Train)** - $12 billion, 508km

### Comparison to China
| Metric | India | China |
|--------|-------|-------|
| Annual infra spending | $100B+ | $1T+ |
| High-speed rail | 500km | 40,000km |
| Expressways | Growing fast | Leader |
