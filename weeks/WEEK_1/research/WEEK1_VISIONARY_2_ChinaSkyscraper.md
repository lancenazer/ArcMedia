## KEY FACTS & DATA

### Xiong'an New Area (The City Built in 2 Years)
- **Location:** 100km southwest of Beijing, Hebei Province
- **Announced:** April 2017
- **Size:** 1,200 km² (largest new city in China)
- **Planned Investment:** $580 billion+
- **Planned Population:** 2-3 million eventually

### Construction Speed Comparison
| Country | Avg. skyscraper build time | Notes |
|---------|---------------------------|-------|
| USA | 4-7 years | Permits, labor, regulations |
| China | 1-2 years | Government support, labor, prefabrication |
| Xiong'an | 2 years (phase 1) | From farmland to partial operation |

### China's Construction Innovation
1. **Modular Prefabrication:** 80%+ of building components factory-made
2. **3D Printing:** Complex architectural elements
3. **AI/Automation:** Robotic construction
4. **Government Speed:** Land acquisition, permits, funding = fast
