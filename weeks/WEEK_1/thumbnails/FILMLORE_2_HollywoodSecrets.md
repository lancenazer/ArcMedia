- **Main Visual:** Hollywood sign (LA landmark) + shocked celebrity face
- **Text (red, bold):** "THEY DON'T WANT YOU TO KNOW"
- **Extra Element:** Exposed document graphic + exclamation mark
- **Color Palette:** Red/black/yellow, mysterious, scandal, conspiracy

## Requirements
- Mysterious, conspiracy-adjacent feel
- Text must be readable (may need shorter version)
- Emotion: curiosity, intrigue, danger

- NOTE: This format is evergreen - works for ANY scandal

"The truth they don't want you to know" is proven evergreen format. Use contrast (dark/red) to make it serious. The Hollywood sign establishes location. Shocked face adds human reaction.
