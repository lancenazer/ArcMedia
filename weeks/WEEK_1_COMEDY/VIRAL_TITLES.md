## TITLE PATTERN BREAKDOWN

### Original Viral Formats:

| Format | Example | Why It Works |
|--------|---------|--------------|
| **Time + ABSOLUTELY + [Action]** | "15 Minutes of Rodney Dangerfield at His ABSOLUTELY Funniest!" | Time certainty + extreme modifier |
| **[Show] Characters Being [ACTION] for [Time]** | "Friends Characters Being SAVAGE for 7 Minutes Straight" | Character reference + action + time + "Straight" |

## KEY ELEMENTS:

1. **Specific Time** - "15 Minutes", "12 Minutes", "7 Minutes", "8 Minutes"
2. **Extreme Modifier** - "ABSOLUTELY Funniest"
3. **Action/Emotion** - "Being SAVAGE", "at His Funniest"
4. **Continuous** - "Straight" implies no cuts
5. **Name Recognition** - "Rodney Dangerfield" / Show characters
