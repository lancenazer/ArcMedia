## KEY PATTERNS TO KEEP

### Format 1 (Use for BEST content):
`[Time] + Minutes of + [Name] + Being + [ADJECTIVE] + [Action] + for + [Time] + Minutes + Straight`

Example: "15 Minutes of Rodney Dangerfield at His ABSOLUTELY Funniest!"

### Format 2 (Use for SHOW/CHARACTER content):
`[Show] + Characters + Being + [ACTION] + for + [Time] + Minutes + Straight`

Example: "Friends Characters Being SAVAGE for 7 Minutes Straight"
