## KEY FACTS & DATA

### The 5 Cities (In This Video's Ranking)

#### #5: Masdar City, UAE
- **Location:** Abu Dhabi
- **Type:** Sustainability/Zero Carbon
- **Size:** 6 km²
- **Founded:** 2010 (first occupants)
- **Status:** Fully operational, expanding
- **Focus:** 100% renewable energy, no carbon footprint

#### #4: Neom (The Line), Saudi Arabia  
- **Location:** Northwestern Saudi Arabia
- **Type:** Linear city / AI-integrated
- **Size:** 26,500 km² (entire region)
- **Status:** Under construction (sections completing 2026)
- **Cost:** $500 billion+

#### #3: Xiong'an, China
- **Location:** 100km SW of Beijing
- **Type:** Smart City / Tech Hub
- **Size:** 1,200 km²
- **Status:** Phase 2 construction
- **Cost:** $580 billion

#### #2: Songdo International Business District, South Korea
- **Location:** Incheon, near Seoul
- **Type:** Fully integrated smart city
- **Size:** 6 km² (complete)
- **Founded:** 2015 (fully inhabited)
- **Cost:** $40 billion
