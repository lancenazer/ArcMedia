## KEY FACTS & DATA

### Jeddah Tower (Kingdom Tower)
- **Location:** Jeddah, Saudi Arabia
- **Original Height Goal:** 1,000 meters (1 km / 3,280 feet)
- **Current Height (2026):** 600+ meters
- **Original Cost:** $20 billion (estimated)
- **Developer:** Jeddah Economic Company / Kingdom Holding
- **Architect:** Adrian Smith (SOM)

### Construction Timeline
- **Announced:** 2011
- **Construction Start:** 2013
- **Paused:** 2018 (due to financial issues)
- **Resumed:** 2023-2024
- **Target Completion:** 2028 or earlier

### Engineering Challenges Solved
1. **Foundation:** 200+ meter deep piles in sand
2. **Concrete:** Specialized mix for extreme heights
3. **Wind:** Designed to flex, not break (at 150+ km/h winds)
4. **Climate:** Desert heat management
