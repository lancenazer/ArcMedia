## BODY - SECRET 1: The Casting Couch (2 minutes)
**[Visual: Old Hollywood photos, casting studio]**

Secret #1: The casting couch isn't dead. It's just more sophisticated now.

**[Cut to:]** Yes, #MeToo changed things. But power dynamics in Hollywood are worse than ever. Agents still control careers. One "no" and you're done.

**[Cut to:]** Most actors you love have stories they won't tell. The ones who speak up? They get blacklisted. Quietly.

---

## SECRET 2: The Rewrite Culture (2 minutes)
**[Visual: Script pages, director edits]**

Secret #2: Most movies are completely different from what was originally filmed.

**[Cut to:]** Studios test screenings change everything. Characters get killed off. Endings are reshot. And actors sign away their objections in contract fine print.

**[Cut to:]** That movie you loved? The original version might have been completely different — and better.

---

## SECRET 3: The Pay Gap (2 minutes)
**[Visual: Red carpet photos]**

Secret #3: The pay gap isn't just about gender. It's about who negotiates.

**[Cut to:]** A-listers get backend deals. Everyone else gets flat fees. Same movie, completely different compensation — even when they're on screen together.

**[Cut to:]** And the streaming era made it worse. No box office profits means no backend checks.

---

## SECRET 4: The Secret Contracts (1 minute)
**[Visual: Legal documents, NDAs]**

Secret #4: Almost every big star has a morals clause. One scandal and they're done.

**[Cut to:]** But here's what's never reported: Studios KNOW about the scandals BEFORE they hire. They calculate the risk. If the movie makes enough money, they sign anyway.

**[Cut to:]** The moral outrage is performative. The money is real.
