## PART 3: THE FORMULA FOR 500K+ VIEWS

### Title Templates (Tested & Proven):

**MegaBuilds:**
1. "The [ADJECTIVE] Project That [VERB] The ENTIRE Country"
2. "[NUMBER] Insane Megaprojects That Will [OUTCOME]"
3. "The TALLEST Building in [COUNTRY] is FINALLY Here"
4. "China/USA/India's MOST [ADJECTIVE] Project"
5. "This $ [X] BILLION Project is CHANGING Everything"

**Military:**
1. "The [WEAPON] That Could [OUTCOME]"
2. "TOP 5 Deadliest [CATEGORY]"
3. "[COUNTRY]'s Secret NEW Weapon"
4. "Why [COUNTRY] is Building This [WEAPON]"
5. "The Most DANGEROUS [WEAPON SYSTEM] in the World"

**Film/Celebrity:**
1. "[CELEBRITY]: The TRUTH They HID"
2. "What Happened to [CELEBRITY] is SHOCKING"
3. "[MOVIE] Behind The Scenes SCANDAL"
4. "The REAL Reason [CELEBRITY] was [FIRED/ARRESTED]"
5. "[CELEBRITY] Lost $ [AMOUNT] — Here's Why"

### Thumbnail Formula:

**Must Have:**
- Face with EMOTION (shocked, angry, surprised)
- Bold uppercase text (3-5 words max)
- High contrast colors (red/yellow/blue on dark)
- No text > 3 words

**Examples:**
- 🔴 Red background + Celebrity face (shocked expression) + "TRUTH REVEALED"
- 🟡 Yellow text on dark + Weapon image + "TOP 5 DEADLIEST"
- 🔵 Blue overlay + Skyscraper + "TALLEST BUILDING"

### Video Length Strategy:

| Niche | Long-Form | Shorts (Discovery) |
|-------|-----------|-------------------|
| MegaBuilds | 12-18 min | 30-60 sec |
| Military | 10-15 min | 30-45 sec |
| Film/Celebrity | 8-12 min | 30-45 sec |

**Key:** Use Shorts to DRIVE to long-form
