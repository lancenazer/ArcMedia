## Content That Outperforms
1. **Specific numbers** — "$X billion" performs better than "billions"
2. **Country flags in thumbnails** — Nationalism drives clicks
3. **Shocked/surprised faces** — Universal emotional hook
4. **Question marks** — Creates curiosity gap
5. **"VS" graphics** — Direct confrontation framing
